About - TreeVest App

According to Just One Tree, approximately the area size of 300,000 football pitches is destroyed every year. The TreeVest App aims at improving environmental conditions by allowing citizens to invest in reforestation. How do we plan on doing this? Easy, for each plant a person plants, they make a dollar investment, which gains 5% interest each year and can be withdrawn after 10 years.
The idea and development of the app is a female-led solution.
